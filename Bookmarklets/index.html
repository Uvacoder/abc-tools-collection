Handy Draggy Bookmarklets

These are some cool bookmarklets in a form where you can drag them to your bookmarks bar without having to manually create them!

Largest Contentful Paint Bookmarklet

"A bookmarklet for viewing the largest contentful paint in a page. Will show each LCP after the bookmarklet is clicked."

This one is by Annie Sullivan and the most updated code can be found in her gist.

Drag this to your bookmarks: LargestContentfulPaint bookmarklet.
